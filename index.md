# Turtini

Independent, open-source–aligned work focused on automation, governance, and operational clarity in complex environments.

This site is the public index for Turtini’s shared resources and community contributions.

---

## Purpose

Turtini publishes practical artifacts intended to be:

- Reusable without heavy customization
- Understandable without insider context
- Safe to reference in professional and public-sector environments

The goal is not volume, but **signal**.

---

## Areas of Focus

### Open Source & Automation
- Ansible- and RHEL-adjacent work
- Infrastructure hardening examples
- Operational patterns that scale calmly

### Governance & Systems Thinking
- AI delegation and usage frameworks

### Red Hat–Aligned Ecosystems
- Content designed to complement official documentation
- Operator-focused guidance grounded in real environments
